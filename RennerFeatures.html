Kerning Pairs
      Renner includes detail-oriented kerning. That means that it includes everything from big, noticeable kerning pairs, such as "AT", to small, subtle kerning pairs, such as "rs". It makes for an overall more refined sense of spacing that works at both small sizes and large.
