Jones*

The American Typeface


Jones* is under construction!

      Jones* is currently being rewritten from the ground up! The version that appears in this page has issues. The “book” weight is too thin, the kerning is weird and inconsistent, letterforms and stoke widths are inconsistant, and there is no italic version. The next version of Jones* is being worked on here and hopes to address all of these issues.

Timeless and Friendly

      Jones* is designed with the ideal, underlying letterform in mind. The result is a robust, approachable, sans-serif, that is at home almost anywhere. This version of Jones* is built off of Julieta Ulanovsky’s Montserrat. Both fonts are licensed under the SIL open type license.
