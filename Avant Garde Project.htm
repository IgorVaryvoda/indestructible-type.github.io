 AGP138 is a transcription of an LP released on Nixa Records (TRB 18400) in 1951. It features five verbal and four instrumental performances of works by composer Lynda Alden. Alden's concept of Trickism is presented through each of the pieces. The transcription is clean, save some tracking distortion in the latter half of the ninth track. Any other apparent record artifacts are present, I believe, intentionally. Although the LP is entitled "Ten Explorations in Trickism" both the record and track listing only include nine tracks [no comment on intentionality]. The text file includes the original liner notes.
