The American Typeface


Beautifully Nondescript

      Jones* was crafted with one goal: to create an invisible typeface. The solution lies in an underlying letter form. A universal collective alphabet. Each letter of Jones* was created to be familiar, to not stand out. The result is a marvelous collection of letters that are simple in geometry, trustworthy, and so basic that they feel as if they have always been.

Timeless and Friendly

      Jones* is named after Tobias Frere-Jones, creator of the iconic Gotham typeface from which Jones* is most informed. Jones* is also informed by the American Type Founder’s Blair, Julieta Ulanovsky’s Montserrat and countless others. Jones* is created from scratch to work well in a variety of places.
