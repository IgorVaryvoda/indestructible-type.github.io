About


A Font Foundry by People for People

      indestructible type* is a font foundry like no other. That is because it operates under the open source model. The fonts available at indestructible type* are created by designers in need of good free typography to be shared with the public freely. Every step behind the creation process is public. We believe that in making our process open we are supporting the creations of even better, more versatile fonts. All of our typography is free to modify, personally or commercially.

Timeless and Friendly

      Jones* is designed with the ideal, underlying letterform in mind. The result is a robust, approachable, sans-serif, that is at home almost anywhere. This version of Jones* is built off of Julieta Ulanovsky’s Montserrat. Both fonts are licensed under the SIL open type license.
